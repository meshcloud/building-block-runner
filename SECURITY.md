# Security Policy

## Dependency Management & Vulnerabilities

We take security seriously and maintain a rigorous approach to dependency management. All dependencies are continuously monitored for known vulnerabilities, and security updates are applied promptly to address any identified issues. meshcloud maintains internal processes and tooling to ensure vulnerabilities are caught early and remediated efficiently.

In the near future, we will introduce a public dependency vulnerability management system to provide greater transparency into our security practices and vulnerability remediation timeline.

For any security concerns or questions about our dependency management practices, please contact our support team at support@meshcloud.io.

## Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please **do not open a public issue**. Instead, email us privately at support@meshcloud.io.

Include:

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if available)
